# 🍪 Download Local Youtube Cookies

Downloading session Youtube cookies from your browser allows the app to authenticate, bypass bot detection, and access age-restricted videos. However, cookies must be refreshed periodically.

1. Install the <a href='https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/cookie-editor/hlkenndednhfkekhgcdicdfddnkalmdm'>Cookie-Editor</a> extension.

2. Log into Youtube and export the cookies in <b>Netscape</b> format.

3. Paste the cookies into the <a href='../Media/Others/Youtube_Cookies.txt'>Youtube_Cookies.txt</a> file.

4. Disable the Cookie-Editor extension.
